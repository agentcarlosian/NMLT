# Code of Conduct

NMLT is a technical research community. Participants must be respectful,
specific, and honest about uncertainty.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, deliberate deception, and
personal attacks are not acceptable. Critique claims, models, proofs, code, and
evidence rather than people. Treat mistakes as opportunities to strengthen the
system, while distinguishing good-faith error from repeated misrepresentation.

Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or ban participants to
protect the community. Report conduct concerns privately to a maintainer.
